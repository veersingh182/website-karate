Opportunities

Our mission is to provide students of NIT Hamirpur a martial art experience that is more than just physical exercise. We provide our students a peaceful environment to develop traits like self-discipline, camaraderie, leadership skills and to learn techniques like Self-defense, unarmed combat, survival tactics and calmness of mind.

  • Chance to learn karate, taekwondo, Indian martial arts and boxing.
  • Chance to represent the institute in various state/national level championships.
  • Earning belt grading from World karate Association.
  • Sword training, Nunchuks training, unarmed combat, close quarter battle and other weapon training.
  • Opportunity to be a part of the academy sword drill on August 15th and January 26th.

Selection Procedure

The selection takes place during 1st semester after a week of 15 August. First Year students of NIT Hamirpur are eligible for Enrolment in the Academy. The Selection process is based on physical and visual standards as well as the overall personality of the students. Following tests are conducted in order to select the best cadets. Physical Fitness test: It is conducted by the Rank Holders in coordination with Faculty In charge of Academy. Candidates are tested on the basis of their stamina, strength and overall fitness

Interview: Students who clear their PFT are supposed to appear for their personal interview in which overall personality of the candidates is assessed and suitable candidates are selected. The final selection list is announced after verifying all the above-mentioned points. Attendance Criteria: Even after selection of students, their monthly attendance shall decide if they will be regular part of training program and other events of the academy. Minimum 80% attendance in each month of training is mandatory.
