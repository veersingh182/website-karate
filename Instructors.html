Instructors

academic year 2018-2020

Gaurav Pratap Sharma

founder and first coordinator, instructor at Martial arts karate academy NIT Hamirpur . A senior black belt in taekwondo and 2nd dan, black belt in karate, he has participated in various national and international tournaments including South Asian Hakuakai Karate Championship 2018 where he won gold medal. Inspired by Akshay kumar, he started practicing martial arts at his home and soon joined martial arts academy in his home town and trained under Mr. Pawan Kumar Sharma to get trained in both taekwondo and karate. Mr. Gaurav Sharma has also been trained in unarmed combat and close quarter battle and has practiced shadow boxing, sword fight, archery, air gun shooting at his own. He was amused to see his sport not being practiced in the college so he established the Martial Arts Karate Academy NIT Hamirpur . He graduated from the institute in the year 2020 with a bachelor’s degree in electrical engineering.

Amit kumar Barnwal

first deputy coordinator of the academy. He is a black belt in karate and an NCC cadet. He played a huge role in establishing the academy and has since trained many cadets while being in the college. Mr. Amit kumar won silver medal in South Asian Hakuakai Karate Championship 2018 . He completed his bachelor’s degree in electrical engineering in the year 2020..

academic year 2020-2021

 Varun Kumar Baliyan

Varun Kumar Baliyan has trained in the academy for 4 years and earned black belt. He was the coordinator of the Academy in the session 2020-21. Being highly active in sports activities, practicing yoga for years and being cadet captain in NCC, he has trained the cadets of the academy in the best way possible with the help of his experience in martial arts. He holds a Bachelor’s degree in chemical engineering (2021).

academic year 2021-2022

 Abhijeet Kumar

black belt in karate and has been practicing martial art for four years, currently in the final year of electrical engineering. Abhijeet joined the academy in his first year, trained under Mr. Gaurav Sharma and experienced the journey from being a white belt to a black belt while being in the college. He also won gold medal in under 60 kg category of Inter-year kumite championship 2020 .
