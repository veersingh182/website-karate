Mission

Our mission is to provide students of NIT Hamirpur a martial art experience that is more than just physical exercise. We provide our students a peaceful environment to develop traits like self-discipline, camaraderie, leadership skills and to learn techniques like Self-defense, unarmed combat, survival tactics and calmness of mind.

Vision

At our Academy, we envision to improve student's lives with the help of martial art. We train to build confidence and strength in the body and mind. we believe that physical and mental well-being are interconnected and anyone can learn martial art irrespective of their gender, background and physicality and change their lives.

History

Martial Arts Karate Academy NIT Hamirpur, is driven by the zeal of its Founder Gaurav Pratap Sharma. The journey of the establishment of the Academy was full of hurdles and adventures. But those obstacles were nothing in comparison to the determination and dedication of the founder. There is a saying that “If opportunity doesn't knock, build a door”, he indeed opened the door of opportunities for the students of NIT Hamirpur to excel in martial Arts and self-defense techniques along with their engineering studies. The journey of the foundation of Martial Arts Karate Academy NIT Hamirpur began around 2016 when Gaurav Sharma started training many students, unofficially though but effectively. Months of efforts resulted in the administration taking note of the efforts and achievements of the students. 3 May 2018, was the red-letter day to pave the way for the establishment of the Academy as the astounding foundation of the Academy took place on this propitious day by the unforgettable efforts of founders of the academy along with the support of administration. On 3 May 2018, an official order was issued by the authorities of NIT Hamirpur stating “Mr. Gaurav Sharma, is designated as the Student Coordinator (Karate), who will initiate the process of setting up of Martial Arts Karate Academy as well as conduct of Self Defense Classes at NIT Hamirpur in consultation with Associate Professor (Physical Education) of the Institute”. On the day of 14 February 2019, director of the institute inaugurated the dojo hall at Central Block of NIT Hamirpur. The dojo hall was allocated to the Academy for the conduction of classes of the Karate Cadets. All the efforts, the founders and supporters gave for establishing the Martial Arts Karate Academy NIT Hamirpur proved worth it. From the beginning of the Academy conduction of karate classes takes place in the academy dojo hall and NIT Hamirpur ground. During the class, cadets show their josh, strength, and perseverance. The aura and energy during the practice session mesmerizes the surroundings. The journey that has started will never stop and will go on driven by its mission and vision.
